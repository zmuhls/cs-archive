Cleveland Union School (Oswego County)

El H. 1895 - admitted
2 Je 1922 - inspection requested - following letter of complaint
24 Je 1922 - Report of inspection shows excessive tax rate; work should be limited to 3 yrs; additional teacher needed if 4th yr is continued.
11 Ag 1923 - additional teacher employed; c or submitted
2 Jl 1926 - app adv S to H - inspection requested; teachers programs overcrowded; c of s critisized.

8 Jl 1926 - criticisms re lists - all will have to be met before adv is recommended.
8 D 1926 - app for adv S-H denied at present
25 Mar 1927 - May 17, 1927 - corr re consolidation
2 Nov 1927 - school not ready for advance
16 Ja 1928 - Mr McComick to visit school
16 F 1928 - report on adv M-3 to H - decision deferred - bldg unfit for occupancy, etc.
