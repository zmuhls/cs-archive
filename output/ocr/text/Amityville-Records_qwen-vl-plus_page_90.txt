The Associated School Boards and Trustees of the State of
New York in a communication dated October 21, 1931 reported
that there was considerable objection to the present dates for
holding the annual meeting and recommended that some date
during the month of June he designated and made uniform for all
districts and asked that the Board of Education record its
wishes on a ballot furnished for that purpose.
