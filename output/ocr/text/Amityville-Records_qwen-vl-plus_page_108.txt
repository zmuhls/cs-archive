On motion made, seconded and carried, it was resolved that the
Boy Scouts of America be granted permission to use the High
School auditorium on the evening of February 14th.

Mr. Ruff reported that with the approval of the President he had
purchased three tables and nine chairs at a cost of $165.00.

Mr. Ruff reported on the need of additional textbooks and
recommended that as the budget allowance for textbooks had
been practically spent that authorization be given to buy books
amounting to $200.00 over the budget appropriation. Moved,
seconded and carried, that Mr. Ruff was authorized to purchase
text books in the amount stipulated in the budget.
