Clinton Heights Union Free School District  
No. 3, Town of East Greenbush  

8 D 1933 - app received for admission M-1  

22 June 1934 - Action deferred on recommendation for admission. Voted that case be referred to G.M.W. with suggestion that he try to work out plan for a central high school and that he make some arrangement for transporting the pupils.  

27 July 1934 - Action deferred pending further consideration, on the recommendation for  

admission of Clinton Heights Union Free School as of Middle grade (1 year)  

25 Jan. 1935 - Admitted to University. Voted That Clinton Heights Union Free School Dist. No. 3, Town of East Greenbush, be admitted to the University as a school of middle high school grade (1 year) for a period of two years, with the distinct understanding that during those two years this community will take such steps as may be necessary to establish a central high school serving the districts interested in that neighborhood.  

19 Sept. 1941 - dropped from roll
