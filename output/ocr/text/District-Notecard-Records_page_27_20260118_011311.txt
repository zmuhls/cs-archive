Ossining Union School  
19 Oct. 1901 admitted as Ossining Union School  
Reduced to junior - cent. sent (morning)  

8 Mar. 12 Advanced to middle - cent. sent (morning)  

23 Sept. 1913 Recommended for adv. on favourable report of Mr. Longdon.  
25 Sept. 1913 Advanced to senior grade.  

11 Nov. 1915 adverse report on little advanced  

Apr. 1926 name changed to Hendrick Hudson Union School  
(School not ready for advancement)  
Building too small  

28 Dec. 1928 - advanced 17-3 to H
