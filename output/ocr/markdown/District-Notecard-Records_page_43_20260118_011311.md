## Transcription

Constableville Free School (Lewis Co.)
1 Mar. 1897 - admitted
6 July 1912 - appl for adv. received
attention called to deficiencies
5 Oct. 1928 - appl for adv. M-2 to M-3
20 Feb. 1929 - Inspection report showed
school was not ready for advancement
27 Mar. 1929 - President of Board and Dist.
Sept. notified.
28 May 1929 - sent blank for appl for adv. to
high school grade

Jun 13/29 - Advanced M-2 to M-3
23 D. 1929 - Advancement M-3 to H
8 Ja. 1930 - favorable report of Inspection
20 Mar. 1930 - advanced to H
Mar 15/61 - name changed from
Constableville U.S. to Constableville
Central School, Constableville

[stamp: Lewis County]
