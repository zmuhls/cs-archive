## Transcription

Bombay Union School

May 21, 1903 - admitted
Oct. 3, 1908 - adv. to S.
6/25-26/59 dropped from roll of registered scholars

[stamp: Bombay Union School]

Oct. 1926 - rec'd app. adv. grade - 5-4 yr.
Inspection to be made
Comp. library rec'd - not complete
During - Inspection report not satisfactory - sent to Schoolm'n's mother

10/1/27 - report of inspection transmitted to Pres't.
Lack of library - Proper equipment - scarce of study
6/27/28 - advanced from M-3 to H grade
