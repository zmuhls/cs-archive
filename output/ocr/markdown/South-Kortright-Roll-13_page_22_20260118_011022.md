## Transcription

April the 11th 1826

At the annual meeting of School District No 2 for the purpose of taking [?] for said District
Voted that Amos Giffin Moderator
Voted that Duncan McDonald be Clerk and Collector

James Stewart
Isaac Bennett Trustees
James Grant

Voted that the by laws stand as they now are
in the year 1824
Foted that this meeting be adjourned till the
Second Tuesday of April one thousand eight
hundred and twenty seven
Duncan McDonald Clerk
