## Transcription

Burdett Union School (Schuyler Co.)

27 Je 1898 - admitted
14 N. 1907 - advanced to 8 gr.
29 S 1915 - letter from clerk, board re dropping third year.
28 May 1919 - recommended to Regents that official notice be sent school to show cause why school should not be demoted - notice sent Je 6.
18 Ag 1920 - recommended demotion
3 S 1920 - demoted from 8 to M (no corr. prior to 1928)

28 Ja 1928 - inspection requested
19 Feb. 1929 - report of inspection - several recommendations made to better school

[stamp: Schuyler Co. School District]
