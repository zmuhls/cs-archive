## Transcription

Bolton Union School, Bolton Landing (Warren Co.)

16 May 1905 - inspector given lists of books and apparatus for correction
22 D 1905 - Inspection asked
27 Je 1905 - C of S approved
30 Je 1906 - Admitted as J
4 D 1906 - applica for advance rec'd - c of s O.K.
25 S 1913 - advanced to middle grade
18 Ja 1914 - app for advance to $18 S 1914 - report of Inspection favorable
24 Je 1915 - advanced M to S

19 F 1931 - app for adv to H rec'd (N)
11 Ap 1931 - Impossible to act at present - on application for advancement

Jan. 22, 1953 - name changed to Bolton Union
Aug 24, 1953 - adv. to full four year h. a. grade $8.50
Sept 23, 1983 - renewed as H.S. for 5 years by [illegible] vote

[stamp: Common School District]
