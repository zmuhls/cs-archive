## Transcription

After a general discussion of the proposition it was moved,
duly seconded and carried, that the Board hold a public
meeting on Thursday, March 19, 1931, at 8:00 O'clock P. M.,
for the purpose of receiving expressions of opinions
concerning the proposed grammar school building.
