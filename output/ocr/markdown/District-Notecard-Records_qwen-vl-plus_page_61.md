## Transcription

South Fallsburg Union School  
(Sullivan County)  

29 May 1929 - sent blanks for admission  
21 July 1929 - app received for admission as M-2  
21 Jan 1930 - corr with prin. re principalship  
11 Jan. 1930 - advised re lack in library and  
apparatus  
8 Apr. additional apparatus list rec'd (N)  
19 Je 1930 - admitted as M-2  
16 Apr 1931 - asked up for rev M-2 to H  
(no lists rec'd - N Dr Blamer to effect)  
14 May 1931 - action deferred until fall  

10 June 1936 - app for reclassification  
from M-2 to H(6-6)  
13 Oct. 1936 - Cannot be considered  
for reclassification  
17 Nov. 1936 - report of inspection  
21 D 1936 - letter to principal - report does  
not justify approval of reclassification  
21 May 1937 - Advanced to M-3(9-11 yrs)  
20 June 1942 - Advanced To H (9-12)
