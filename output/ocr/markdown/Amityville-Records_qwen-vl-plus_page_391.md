## Transcription

Motion duly made, seconded and carried, that the action of
Mr. Vogel in purchasing the school supply as requested by
Mr. Brown and Mr. Ruff, be and is hereby ratified.

On motion duly made, seconded and carried, it was resolved
that school open on Wednesday, September 8th.

There being no further business, the meeting thereupon
adjourned.

C. F. Warren
Clork.
