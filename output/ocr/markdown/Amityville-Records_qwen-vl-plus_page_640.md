## Transcription

```
school district a sum sufficient to pay the principal and interest
of said bonds as the same respectively become due and payable.

FURTHER RESOLVED, that when said bonds shall have
been duly executed the same shall be delivered to the purchaser upon
payment of the purchase price to the treasurer, and the receipt
of the treasurer shall be a full acquittance to the purchaser who
shall not be obliged to see to the application of the purchase
money.

The question of the adoption of the foregoing resolution
was duly put to a vote which resulted as follows:
Ayes: 5
Nays: 0

The President thereupon declared the resolution duly adopted.

There being no further business, the meeting thereupon
adjourned.

C. R. Werren.
Clerk.
```
