[typed:]  
6.  
State of New York  
County of Allegany.  

George W. Russell of Wellsville in said  
County of Allegany being duly sworn says that he acted as clerk  
at the several meetings above mentioned and that the foregoing are true copies of  
the minutes kept at said meeting respectively and that the same  
are respectively a true history of the proceedings of said meet-  
ings.  

Sworn and subscribed this  
15th day of November, 1861  
before me  
D. L. Vaughn, Justice  
of the Peace.  

Geo. W. Russell  

[handwritten:]  
Wellsville  
Allegany County
