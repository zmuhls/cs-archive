The Common School Archive contains three primary curated collections documenting the history of public education in New York State.

## [District Consolidation Records](/cs-archive/collections/district-consolidation-by-county/)

**116 pages from 29 New York counties**

School district consolidation tables from NYS Archives Series A4456, documenting the reorganization of rural school districts across New York State. Includes data on:

- Union Free Schools
- Town School Units
- Consolidated Districts
- Central Rural Schools

[View on Map](/cs-archive/map/) | [Browse by County](/cs-archive/collections/district-consolidation-by-county/)

## [NYS Teachers' Association](/cs-archive/collections/nys-teachers-association/)

**Spanning 1845 to the 1940s**

Historical records from the New York State Teachers' Association, including:

- Annual meeting proceedings (16 locations across NYS)
- Membership and organization materials
- Legislative advocacy documents
- Publications and periodicals

[View Meeting Locations](/cs-archive/map/) | [Browse Collection](/cs-archive/collections/nys-teachers-association/)

## [NYS Archives Local District Records](/cs-archive/collections/nys-local-records/)

**19th and early 20th century governance materials**

Primary source materials from NYS Archives documenting local school district administration, including:

- Meeting minutes and administrative records
- District notecards and documentation
- Governance materials from Series A4645, B0594, A4456

[Browse Collection](/cs-archive/collections/nys-local-records/)

## About the Collections

These collections were digitized from archival materials held at the NYS Archives and other repositories. Documents have been processed using multimodal AI (Qwen VL Plus) for transcription, classification, and metadata extraction.

Each artifact page includes:
- High-resolution source images
- Full OCR transcription
- Document classification and metadata
- Confidence scores for transcription accuracy
