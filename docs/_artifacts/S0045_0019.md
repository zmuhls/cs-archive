Quotations from the Writings of Horace Mann

Horace Mann's dedication to the cause of the free public school as an instrument for lifting the lives of people, and for the elevation of society found eloquent expression in his writings. He was a careful observer, thought deeply, wrote or spoke with might on a wide variety of topics.

The following quotations may be used in bronze plaques, in school parades, on schoolroom walls, in editorials in the local newspaper, and in other ways.
