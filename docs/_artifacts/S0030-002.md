It seeks to achieve definite aims which will result in better conditions for teachers, advance the standards for the profession and a more effective service in the public schools. Because of this the officers have set the minimum of 30,000 members for the coming year. We shall depend upon your schools to be in the class of 30,000. Help the Association grow!

What the State Teachers' Association has done for the Teachers of New York State

1. It has given the teachers of this State the best Teachers' Retirement Law in the world for teachers.
2. It has secured a minimum salary law for teachers.
3. It has secured greatly increased quotas from the State and thus en- ables local boards of education to raise salaries.
4. It secured the passage of the law giving increasing academic quotas.
5. It defeated the bill which had for its object the taking away from rural teachers the quotas of their expenses in attending conferences.
6. It secured the abolition of the military training law.
