```
Notice

A special school meeting of the electors of joint common school district number four (4) of the Towns of Colesville, Broome County, and Afton, Chenango County, N.Y., will be held at the school house Tuesday evening, June 1, 1915, for the purpose of:—

1—Determining whether or not a Union Free School District shall be formed in said district number four (4).

2—Receiving report of committee on school house site, plans and specifications for a new school building and approximate cost of same, and acting upon such report.

TRUSTEES.

F.M. Clift
Ewrett

Harperville, N.Y.
May 11, 1915
```
