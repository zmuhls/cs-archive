New York State Teachers' Association  
OFFICERS, 1892-93-94.  

President.  
George E. Hardy, Grammar School, No. 82, 1st Ave. and 70th St., N. Y.  

Vice-Presidents.  
S. G. Williams, Wayland E. Stearns, Mrs. J. F. Hopkins, Jennie B. Brook, Cornell University, Mohawk, Brooklyn, Elmira, Cortland, Hamilton.  

Secretary.  
WELLAND HENDRICK.  

Assistant Secretary.  
C. H. VAN TUYL.  

Transportation Agent.  
ARTHUR COOPER, 3 East 14th St., New York.  

Treasurer.  
PERCY I. BUGBEE.  

Superintendent of Exhibits.  
E. C. COLBY, 13 Exchange St., Rochester.  

Executive Committee.  
GEORGE E. HARDY, Chairman, Ex-Officio. C. STEBBINS, BROOKLYN. C. F. WHEELLOCK, ALBANY. JAMES M. CASSETTY, BUFFALO. M. J. MICHAEL, Ft. PLAIN. E. N. JONES, PLATTSBURGH. I. E. YOUNG, NEW ROCHELLE.  

Terms Expire 1894. Terms Expire 1895. Terms Expire 1896.
