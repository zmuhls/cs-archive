ROESSEVILLE SCHOOL  
Stop 28 Schenectady Road  
Albany New York  

February 21, 1930  

Notice to taxpayers and legal voters, Common School District No. 2, Town of Colonie, County of Albany.  

Formal notice is hereby given of a special public meeting of all legal voters of Common School District No. 2, Town of Colonie, to be held in the auditorium of the school Friday evening March 21, 1930 at 7:30 o'clock for the purpose of adopting or rejecting a resolution, duly proposed, to change the designation of this district from "Common School" to "Union Free School". No other business except that which shall naturally and necessarily result from your action on the proposed resolution may be considered at that meeting.  

You are authorized and directed to convey by word of mouth, this notification to all other voters in the district.  

THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES  

For the Board  

[signature: Q.D. Kairmes]  
clerk  

[stamp: Official]
