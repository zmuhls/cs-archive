```
Deposit, N.Y. June 1910.

To Whom it May Concern:-

WHEREAS, a special meeting of the electors of the joint common
school district number four (4) of the Towns of Colesville and Apton
was duly called and met on June 1, 1915 and voted a resolution that the
said common school district be changed to a Union Free School district
THEREFORE, I Kasson E. Beilby, District Superintendent of schools
having jurisdiction over the school in said district, HEREBY
designate said district as Joint Union Free School District Number
Four (4) of the Towns of Colesville and Apton.

Kasson E. Beilby
District Superintendent of Schools of the
First Supervisory District of Broome County.

[stamp: Deposit, N.Y. June 1910.]

Attach to papers
```
