Local Organization, Rochester, N. Y.

OFFICERS.

President.—Mark W. Way, Grammar School No. 20, Oakman Street, Rochester, N. Y.

Vice-Presidents.—George H. Walden, Sarah Shelton, Sietske Hoekstra, Mary E. Colburn, Helen F. Samaine, George F. Forbes, A. L. Osborn, Matilda Oswald, Nellie F. Cornell, Margaret H. Johnston, Alida Lattimore, Zenas Westervelt, Eben Rose.

Secretary.—Charles D. Blackmon, 69 Platt Street.

Treasurer.—Richard A. Searing, 478 Alexander Street.

Superintendent of Exhibits.—Frank A. Reichenbach.

Executive Committee:

Chairman committee on halls and spaces.—Ezra M. Sparlin.

Chairman committee on entertainment.—Samuel P. Moulethrop.

Chairman committee on reception.—Samuel C. Pierce.

Chairman committee on printing and press.—Rich'd A. Searing.

Chairman committee on music.—Julius L. Townsend.

Chairman committee on transportation.—Chas. D. Blackmon.

Chairman committee on finance.—Mark W. Way.

[stamp: Rochester, N.Y. 1885]
