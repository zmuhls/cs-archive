The New York State Teachers' Association

Delegates has adopted a zoning system whereby the State is divided into seven zones or districts. These districts and the meetings for the present year are as follows:

Northern District
Franklin and St. Lawrence Counties
Meeting place POTSDAM on Thursday and Friday, October 7-8, 1926.
Officers: President, Carlos S. Blood, District Superintendent of Schools, Hurlbuton; Vice-President, Gertrude E. Hyde, District Superintendent of Schools, Mora; Secretary-Treasurer, Charles Van Hoesen, Principal Junior High School, Potsdam Normal School.

Western District
Cattaraugus, Chautauqua, Erie, Genesee and Niagara Counties
Meeting place ROCHESTER on Thursday and Friday, November 4-5, 1926.
Officers: President, Dr. F. Darling, Superintendent of Schools, Utica; Vice-President, Dr. Harry Rockwell, Principal Buffalo State Normal School; Secretary-Treasurer, Mrs. D. B. Connolly, District Superintendent of Schools, Ashville.

Central-Western District
Allegany, Livingston, Monroe, Ontario, Orleans, Wayne, Wyoming and Yates Counties
Meeting place ROCHESTER on Thursday, November 4-5, 1926.
Officers: President, W. E. Hawley, Principal Monroe Junior High School, Newark; Vice-President, N. F. Strawn, District Superintendent of Schools, Fulton; Secretary-Treasurer, Kathryn O. Ward, Principal School No. 39, Rochester.

Central District
Cayuga, Hamilton, Herkimer, Jefferson, Lewis, Madison, Oneida, Onondaga, Oswego and Seneca Counties
Meeting place UTICA on Thursday and Friday, October 21-22, 1926.
Officers: President, Miss Mabel E. Harris, Principal Franklyn School, Utica; Vice-President, G. R. Bodley, Superintendent of Schools, Poughkeepsie; Secretary-Treasurer, Miss Anna M. Chapman, Department of Public Instruction, Utica.

Eastern District
Albany, Clinton, Columbia, Dutchess, Essex, Greene, Rensselaer, Saratoga, Schenectady, Schoharie, Ulster, Warren and Washington Counties
Meeting place ALBANY on Thursday and Friday, October 21-22, 1926.
Officers: President, E. R. Coubon, School No. 12, Robbins Sc., Washington Bird; Vice-President, Anna Know, Department of Public Instruction, Poughkeepsie; Secretary-Treasurer, G. M. Elmdorf, Superintendent of Schools, Oneonta.

Southern District
Broome, Chemung, Chenango, Cortland, Delaware, Otsego, Schuyler, Steuben, Tioga and Tompkins Counties
Meeting place BINGHAMTON on Thursday and Friday, November 11-12, 1926.
Officers: President, George J. Dann, Superintendent of Schools, Binghamton; Vice-President, Robert E. Knapp, Superintendent of Schools, Oneonta; Secretary-Treasurer, Edith Tollerion, Colleics.

Southeastern District
Nassau, Orange, Putnam, Rockland, Sullivan and Westchester Counties
Meeting place NEW YORK CITY on Thursday and Friday, October 14-15, 1926.
Officers: President, A. H. Naylor, Superintendent of Schools, Port Jervis; Vice-President, Fred J. Lewis, Superintendent of Schools, Barryville; Secretary-Treasurer, Ervin.

HOUSE OF DELEGATES MEETING AT UTICA,
Monday and Tuesday, November 22-23, 1926.
